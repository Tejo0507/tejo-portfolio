## Optimize Your CV for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)

1. **Use standard section headings**
   - "Experience" not "My Journey"
   - "Education" not "Academic Background"

2. **Use standard fonts**
   - Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman

3. **Avoid images, graphics, tables**
   - They confuse ATS systems

4. **Use keywords from job descriptions**
   - AI, Machine Learning, Python, etc.

5. **Simple formatting**
   - Bullet points
   - Clear hierarchy
   - Consistent spacing
